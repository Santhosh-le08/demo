The Global Food Waste Crisis

Every year, approximately 1.3 billion tons of food is wasted globally, which is about one-third of all food produced for human consumption. This waste has significant environmental, economic, and social impacts:

  • Food waste contributes to 8% of global greenhouse gas emissions
  • Nearly 690 million people go hungry every day
  • Food waste costs the global economy $940 billion annually
  • 25% of the world's fresh water supply is used to grow food that is never eaten

Our Solution

FoodWise is an AI-powered platform that helps individuals and organizations reduce food waste through smart inventory management and predictive analytics:

  • AI-powered inventory tracking and expiry date monitoring
  • Smart notifications for items approaching expiration
  • Automated shopping list generation based on consumption patterns
  • Waste reduction analytics and insights
  • Recipe suggestions based on available ingredients
